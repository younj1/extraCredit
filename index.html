Elon Musk

Early Life

Elon Musk was born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa. His father was an electromechanical engineer, and his mother was a nutritionist and model.

Education

Elon Musk attended the University of Pretoria for a few months before moving to the United States to attend the University of Pennsylvania, where he received degrees in physics and economics. He later attended Stanford University for a PhD in energy physics, but dropped out after just two days to start his first company.

Career

Elon Musk is known for his work in the tech industry, particularly with his companies PayPal, Tesla, and SpaceX. He co-founded PayPal in 1999 and sold it to eBay in 2002. He founded Tesla in 2003 and has since revolutionized the automotive industry with his electric cars. He also founded SpaceX in 2002 with the goal of making space travel more accessible and affordable.

Personal Life

Elon Musk has been married three times and has six children. He is known for his passion for space travel and his efforts to make it more accessible to the general public. He has also made headlines for his Twitter presence, where he has been known to make controversial statements and engage in online spats with other users.
